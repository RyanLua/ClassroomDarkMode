Dark mode theme for Google Classroom, saving your eyes for class. For those late nights of studying or early mornings of homework, say goodbye to eye strain and hello to darkness.

Google Classroom is part of Google Workspace for Education and is used by students and teachers. One fatal flaw is its lack of dark mode, this extension aims to fix that by applying a dark theme on Google Classroom.

- Beautifully designed: Designed with Material Design guidelines to fit right in with other Google products
- Fast & Small: With less than 50KB in size and no recordable memory footprint, expect zero slowdowns to your device
- Secure & Private: Minimal permissions required and no data collected or sold ever makes this extension exceptionally safe

To use this extension, install Classroom Dark Mode and refresh Google Classroom.
To disable this extension, go to your browser's extensions page, disable "Classroom Dark Mode", and refresh Google Classroom.

This extension works on both teacher and student accounts on Google Classroom and in 55 different languages.
